Welcome to SM Magnetics

SM Magnetics: Innovating Beyond Traditional Magnetics

SM Magnetics is an innovative and solutions-oriented magnetics company. Our knowledge and expertise is in permanent magnets and magnetic assemblies, but our ability to quickly understand your application and requirements goes beyond your typical magnetics company.

SM Magnetics began in 2014 out of the expansion and growth of the custom magnet and magnetic assembly business of SuperMagnetMan which has been a well-known online magnet resource and retailer for nearly 25 years. With our online retail business SuperMagnetMan, we are able to quickly provide prototyping magnets and magnetic assemblies for testing.
